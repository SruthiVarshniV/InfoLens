InfoLens 
-Analyze any article or URL for bias and credibility. We show the evidence, you decide what to believe.

What it does?
Paste any article or drop a URL and InfoLens will:
- Give a bias score across multiple categories
- Highlight suspicious sentences with reasons
- Write an AI summary explaining what's off and why
- Show fact-check results from trusted databases

Tech Stack
- Frontend — React + Vite
- Backend — Python + FastAPI
- AI — Groq (Llama 3.3 70B)
- Scraping — BeautifulSoup

Why InfoLens?
Most fact-checking tools just say "fake" or "real". InfoLens shows you why something looks suspicious and lets you make your own call. It's a research assistant, not a judge.
